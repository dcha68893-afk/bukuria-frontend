About Us

Our story, our beliefs, and the people who lead us.

Our History

Gwikonge PEFA Church was established to bring the message of hope and salvation to our community. Since then, we have grown into a vibrant, multi-generational family of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and service.

Statement of Faith

We believe in one God, the Holy Trinity; the inspired and authoritative Word of God; salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ; and the empowering work of the Holy Spirit in the life of every believer.

Mission

To proclaim the Gospel, make disciples, and equip believers to live out their faith daily.

Vision

A Spirit-filled, multi-generational church transforming our community through Christ.

Core Values

Worship

Honoring God in spirit and truth.

Discipleship

Growing believers into Christ-likeness.

Service

Serving our community with love.
