About Us

Our story, our beliefs, and the people who lead us.

Our History

Gwikonge PEFA Church was founded on a vision to bring the transforming power of the Gospel to our community. What began as a small fellowship has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational family of believers. Over the years, God has blessed us with growth in numbers, depth of faith, and breadth of ministry impact across Gwikonge and beyond.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • We believe in the inspired, authoritative Word of God
  • We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
  • We believe in the Holy Spirit's empowering work in every believer
  • We believe in the resurrection of the dead and eternal life
  • We believe in the universal Church as the body of Christ
